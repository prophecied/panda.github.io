London Bike Rides

I delved into the London Bike Sharing dataset, a rich 17414x10 dataset directly sourced from Kaggle via their API. Using Python's powerful Pandas library, I skillfully extracted and transformed the data from a zip file, reading it into a CSV format. Striving for clarity and precision, I executed basic changes such as specifying column names and conducting thoughtful renaming. To enhance understanding, I meticulously crafted a dictionary to decode the significance of specific IDs within the dataset. Ensuring data accessibility, I seamlessly wrote the transformed DataFrame to an Excel file. The visualization journey continued with Tableau, where I crafted dynamic and informative dashboards. Here, I demonstrated my proficiency in creating parameterized and calculated fields, pushing the boundaries of data storytelling. Join me on this data exploration voyage, where Python becomes a language for unraveling stories within the numbers, and Tableau transforms data into captivating visual narratives.

Yellow Cab

I navigated the bustling streets of New York City through the lens of a massive dataset comprising 100,000 rows and 17 columns, all centered around the iconic Yellow Cab services. This treasure trove of data was securely stored in Google Cloud Storage, setting the stage for a data odyssey. With the prowess of Mage and the computational muscle of Google Virtual Machines, I meticulously executed the ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) process. The journey continued as I harnessed the analytical might of BigQuery, unraveling key insights through basic analytics. But what's data without a visual story? The narrative truly came to life in Google Looker, where I crafted visually striking and interactive visualizations, providing a dynamic window into the intricate patterns and narratives hidden within the Yellow Cab dataset. Join me on this expedition, where each row and column is a waypoint in the exploration of NYC's Yellow Cab data landscape. Let the data-driven stories unfold!

Movies

Step into the world of cinema through the lens of data! In my latest Python-driven project, I delved into the enchanting realm of movies with a dataset sourced directly from the Kaggle API. Armed with Python's analytical prowess, I meticulously curated the dataset, casting a discerning eye to check for outliers and purge duplications. The journey continued with statistical exploration using Seaborn's regplot, unraveling nuanced relationships within the data. Harnessing the power of correlation analysis, I crafted a comprehensive correlation matrix and translated it into a visually captivating heatmap. But the data exploration didn't stop there; I harnessed the categorical power of factorization and brought insights to life through Seaborn's expressive stripplot. Join me on this cinematic journey, where Python becomes the storyteller, and data paints a vivid picture of the fascinating world of movies.

Covid

Embark on a data-driven journey through the complexities of the global pandemic with my latest project. Leveraging the robust capabilities of Microsoft SQL Server, I navigated the intricacies of a comprehensive COVID dataset. Through a symphony of Joins, Common Table Expressions (CTEs), Temp Tables, and Windows Functions, I unraveled profound insights, transforming raw data into a nuanced narrative. The exploration extended to the visual realm, where Tableau became my canvas. Through meticulous application of Aggregate Functions and the artistry of data visualization, I crafted interactive dashboards that breathe life into the numbers. Join me on this expedition where SQL becomes a language for decoding the pandemic's data intricacies, and Tableau transforms statistics into impactful visual stories.
